    How it works

    1. Admin creates a game tied to a forum thread on lolz.live.
    2. Players post commands in that thread:
      • !join (or !join red / !join blue in team mode)
      • !goto B8 — drive to a cell
      • !shot A1 — fire toward a cell
      • !leave — remove your tank
    3. Tanks appear with the player's lolz nickname and avatar.
    4. Spectators watch the battle live on the game page.
